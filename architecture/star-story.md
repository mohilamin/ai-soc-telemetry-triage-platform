# STAR Story

Situation: SOC teams face noisy alerts and fragmented telemetry.  
Task: Build a local platform that correlates alerts into explainable incidents.  
Action: Generated telemetry, injected attacks, evaluated Sigma-style rules, correlated incidents, scored risk, created runbooks, and exposed API/dashboard views.  
Result: A reproducible cybersecurity data engineering portfolio project for SOC triage and detection quality.
